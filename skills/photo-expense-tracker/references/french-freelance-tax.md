# French Freelance Tax Reference (Photographe Auteur / BNC)

## TVA Rates (2026)

| Rate | Applies to |
|------|-----------|
| 20% | Equipment, software, services, transport, office supplies |
| 10% | Restaurants, prepared food, hotel accommodation |
| 5.5% | Books, publications, cultural events |
| 2.1% | Press publications (newspapers, magazines) |

## Common Deductible Expenses for Photographers

- Camera equipment, lenses, lighting, accessories
- Computer hardware and software (Lightroom, Photoshop, etc.)
- Travel (train, flights, fuel, tolls, parking)
- Meals during assignments (capped at reasonable amount)
- Phone and internet (pro-rata if mixed use)
- Studio/office rent or coworking
- Insurance (professional liability, equipment)
- Printing and lab costs
- Website hosting and domain
- Professional subscriptions and memberships
- Training and workshops
- Shipping and postage
- Bank fees (professional account)

## TVA Recovery

- Freelancers registered for TVA can recover TVA paid on business expenses
- Keep all original receipts (paper or digital) for 6 years minimum
- TVA is recovered via quarterly or monthly declarations (CA3 form)
- Receipts must show: merchant name/SIRET, date, TVA amount, total TTC

## BNC Regime Basics

- **Micro-BNC**: Revenue < €77,700/year — flat 34% expense deduction, no TVA if below threshold
- **Déclaration contrôlée**: Above threshold or by choice — deduct actual expenses, must keep full accounting
- Photographer-authors (artistes-auteurs) may fall under specific MDA/Agessa regime

## Receipt Requirements

Valid receipt for tax purposes must show:
1. Merchant name and address (or SIRET)
2. Date of transaction
3. Itemized list or description
4. Total amount TTC
5. TVA amount and rate (for amounts > €150, merchant SIRET is required)
